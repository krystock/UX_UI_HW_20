tryppy

take the stress out of vacation planning with tryppy




role: ui designer

team size: 5

project duration: 4 weeks

tools: figma, xd, miro




tryppy is a travel planning app that actively helps the user plan their vacation by featuring what works best for their time, budgets, wants, and needs.




research


in this research project, my team and I conducted 6 user interviews and distributed a survey to understand the user's methods in booking their travel plans. Some key questions we wanted to answer were: what were the obstacles when planning a vacation, how often did the user go on vacation, and how did the user make bookings for their destination.
